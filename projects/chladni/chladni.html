Chladni Plate

This simulation visualizes Chladni plate patterns, which are unique vibrational modes on a surface responding to different frequencies. By moving your mouse over the canvas, you can adjust the integers N and M, which define the standing wave pattern solutions to the differential equation governing the plate's vibrations.

To interact with the simulation:
→ Hover your mouse over the canvas to change N and M, revealing new resonance patterns
→ Observe how different integer values create intricate and distinct vibrational shapes on the plate
