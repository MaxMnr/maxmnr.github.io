Vicsek Model

This simulation is inspired by the landmark paper by Vicsek et al. (1995), which introduced a simple model to study the collective behavior of birds. The model features particles moving on a 2D plane at a constant speed, aligning their direction with the average direction of their neighbors based on an Ising-like approach.

To observe the emergence of collective motion, you can adjust:
→ The number of birds
→ The size of the box
→ The bias
